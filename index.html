Why us?

We're not just a team; we're entrepreneurs who've walked the fashion journey ourselves, each of us having had our own brands at some point. With a collective experience of over 50 years, we know firsthand the challenges and pitfalls of the industry. The reason we launched AVmade was due to our conversations often leading to familiar themes: the hurdles of launching a brand, the mediocrity frequently found among factories, and the undeniable obstacles we faced at nearly every turn. And let's not forget the daunting costs associated with the traditional development process. These shared experiences and frustrations led to a pivotal, "Eureka!" moment during one of our discussions.

Armed with our comprehensive grasp of fashion and supported by our tech prowess, we're ushering in a new era with next generation digital manufacturing. Our mission? To redefine processes, level the playing field, and create a space where emerging brands can not only compete but truly thrive.
