CHOOSING SALES CHANNEL

Picking the right model for optimal brand growth and longevity.
You are selling to your customer directly.
Knowing as wholesale.
When launching a brand, your initial focus should be crafting your mission and forging a deep bond with customers. This foundation is crucial for two reasons:

1. Feedback: Direct customer relationships offer invaluable insights to refine your product. Wholesalers, despite their best intentions, are not capable of delivering the candid feedback you need.
2. Brand Ambassadors: Loyal customers will champion your brand, fostering organic growth and network effect.

It might seem ideal to dive into wholesale for what seems like a good sales channel, but it's not for startups. Wholesale as an emerging brand can erode your margins by minus 60-70%. Remember, established brands resort to wholesale once they've maximized their direct-to-consumer potential. Plus, established brands can do large volumes and therefore they get greater manufacturing cost reductions to compensate for their margin. As a startup, prioritize building your foundation. Jumping ahead to wholesale prematurely risks both financial and emotional burnout, jeopardizing your brand's longevity."

DO’s & DON’Ts

Due diligence is critical, don’t rush it.
  • Choose a factory specializing in your niche.
  • Research and compare factories.
  • Look for available incentives.
