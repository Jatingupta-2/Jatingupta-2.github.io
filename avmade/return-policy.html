FACTORIES RETURN POLICY

AVmade Inc. has carved a return policy for all factories to follow when transacting on our platform. All orders are shipped directly from the factories, and buyers' return is sent to the factory instead of AVmade.com. 

 

CLAIM REFUND/REPLACEMENT POLICY

All products are made-to-order according to the brand owner's request and all transactions are final on AVmade. However, we take an active role in every purchase made on AVmade to ensure both the buyer and factories enjoy a great experience. 

 

A buyer can file a claim with AVmade.com if,

  • Factories fail to ship the goods promptly within the promised window.
  • Factories ships a product whose description on our platform does not match
  • Factories ship goods that are damaged.
  • A human error occurred and the wrong postal mark was stuck on the package, in another word the wrong package is sent.

 

A buyer cannot file a claim if,

  • Having buyers remorse or change of mind can be the catalyst for why a factory will go out of business as this is not a game, therefore this is also not acceptable. Note: samples of items are available for purchase to align with customer expectations. 
  • The claim is false or fraudulent. Depending on the case legal action can be taken.

 

A factory can reject the claim,

  • The items produced match as described on AVmade.com. 
  • If a factory has a document to justify a claim that is false or fraudulent. In this case please submit the document immediately for our legal team to revise from your dashboard. 

 

A factory can not reject the claim,

  • If the buyer's reasoning aligns with our policy, quality, and deadline-related issues. 

 

We are highly dedicated to perfecting the brand owners' and factories' experience by tracking every process carefully and working with both buyers and factories to continue closing the gaps for all unforeseen issues both in our short and long-term business practices.

 

CLAIM DEADLINE

Brands have 3 days after receiving the package to file a claim if an issue has occurred. If not based on the pacific time zone, on the 3rd day from the day the package was delivered, funds will be released to the factory. Although we will do our best to help even after the deadline has exceeded our claim policy as a gesture of goodwill, please note legally we'll no longer be responsible nor will we hold the factory responsible. 

 

DOCUMENTS NEED

When a case is opened, the buyer is asked to upload,

A. Photos showing the damages/defects.

B. Description of all issues.

C. The number of defective products, if the issue has only occurred only on a few items.

 

f the return is verified, we assist the buyer in returning the item to the factory and offer them the choice of either a full refund or a replacement with gratitude to give the factory a second chance if agreed. 

 

3 STRIKE POLICY

Please note we have this policy to weed the nonqualified members from being a part of our tribe.

  1. Factories have three strikes. If any grand errors have occurred three times, we will permanently remove the factory.
  2. If a buyer is suspected to abuse our policy or is caught filing a fraudulent claim, then he/she will also be removed permanently.

Please note we have the right to proceed with legal actions for falsification of any kind. To avoid any problems, we demand factories to be as descriptive and detailed as possible when creating listings and we also encourage buyers to review the listing photos, descriptions, and many more details listed on the product page.

 

REFUND/REPLACEMENT POSTMARKS

All returns/replacements must be postmarked within 5 days from the day the return/replacement was approved for ship back. All returned items must be in their original form of packaging with all details intact and unused.

 

RETURN/REPLACEMENT PROCESS

Log in to your AVmade account and on your dashboard, you will find a button labeled "Items Eligible for Claim" when you scroll down at the bottom of your dashboard to begin your claim process. Once your claim is granted, 

  1. Place the item/s securely in its original packaging, including the return/replacement form provided, along with the copy of the original receipt.
  2. Tape the box very well to avoid items from falling out on their way to the factory.
  3. Print a return label from your dashboard and request a pickup.

 

REFUND/REPLACEMENT 

Please provide up to 3 days for the factory to inspect the condition of the items in a claim to issue a refund/replacement. For refunds, please note depending on the credit card provider it can take between 5-10 days to reach your account starting from the day we finalized and okayed the refund.
