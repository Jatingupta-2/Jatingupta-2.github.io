ONLINE DESIGN

UPSCALE
ESSENTIALS

We constantly analyze the upscale market for top-selling basic essentials our brand partners can easily incorporate into their collections. Based on this research, we've curated a library of over 300 innovative products and counting. Since these products have already undergone development, brands can save up to 90% in sample fees. However, please note that the online design studio only offers essentials like tees, hoodies, sweaters, sweatpants, shorts and alike. For online customization options available for these products, please see the list below or visit our website for more details.

  • Custom Color : Choose from over 2,000 color options.
  • Custom Embellishments : Options include printing, embroidery, etc.
  • Custom Main Label : Select between woven or printed brand labels.
  • Custom Hangtag : Tailored to your brand.
  • Custom Treatment : Enhancements like antimicrobial, dry-wicking, etc.
  • Custom Wash : Varieties such as vintage, stone wash, potassium, etc.
  • Custom Packaging : Personalized to your specifications.

If you're looking to create a style with different fits and features not listed above, then our “offline” manufacturing services would be more suitable. For more information on this option, please see below.

In this option, you have the freedom to create a wide range of products. We specialize in contemporary styles and high-quality casual and formal wear, including denim. leather, outdoor gear, athleisure, and accessories like hats and bags. It's important to note that we are U.S.-based and set up specifically to cater to upscale and designer brands. This means if your products are targeted at mid-market pricing, our services may not align with your needs.
