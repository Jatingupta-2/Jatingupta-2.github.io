What’s Full Package Manufacturing?

Full-package garment manufacturing involves transforming a concept from a mere idea into a product ready for mass production. It entails a holistic approach where a manufacturer is responsible for various stages of production, including pattern making, sample creation, fabric sourcing, dyeing, cutting, sewing, finishing, and decoration.

What sets full-package clothing manufacturing apart is its commitment to transparent pricing. With our vast industry experience and robust infrastructure, we offer a wide range of services to our brand partners. It's essential for brands to be discerning. Some manufacturers might not disclose that information, leading to unexpected additional charges. It's not uncommon to initially believe you're paying price X, only to find the actual cost is 2-3 times higher. Should you encounter such situations, we recommend seeking alternative manufacturing partnerships.

For instance look for red flag answers such as:
  • "We can source your fabric, but it'll cost $X/hr."
  • "We can't provide any information until the sample is ready."
  • "There's a consultation fee to inform you."
With AV, all we need is a sample or an image to give us an idea of the required item type and material, and we'll provide you with a transparent quote. If you need more insights or assistance, don't hesitate to get in touch. We're committed to guiding you through the entire process at no extra cost.

Clothing manufacturing cost

To accurately determine the cost of garment manufacturing, we require three key details: the Style Type, Fabric Choice, and Quantity. Think of it in terms of automobiles; the make, detailing, and model are pivotal in setting the price. In a similar vein, for apparel manufacturing, these foundational details enable us to provide a precise cost estimate. Once you've consolidated your ideas onto a vision board, share your concept with us. We'll then provide you with a comprehensive quote. If you have a specific price point in mind for garment production, share that too, and we'll outline a strategy to help you meet those financial targets. However, it's important to note that our expertise lies in crafting products that match the quality standards of contemporary brands. If you're aiming for price points akin to those of lower price points retail chains like Macy's then we may not be the best fit for your brand's needs.
